Ultimate Game Policy

Updated October 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Ultimate Game collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile game.

1. Data We Collect

We collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system, and game progress to improve your gaming experience. We do not collect personally identifiable information like your name or email address directly.

2. In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, transaction details are handled by the Google Play Store and are subject to their privacy policies. We do not store your financial information.

3. Third-Party Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand game usage and performance. These services may collect anonymous data about your device and how you interact with the game.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is not directed to children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the game or on our website.
